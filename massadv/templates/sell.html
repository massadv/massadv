How To Consign

At MassAdvertiser.com, we have one goal in mind. We connect serious sellers with serious buyers. We make it incredibly easy for you to list and sell your items at a price you feel is fair. We offer MassAdvertiser.com as a risk-free alternative to those sellers who are not as comfortable with the inherent risks that are sometimes associated with our AuctionByEmail format.

The process is simple. Take a look at our options and fee schedule below. Choose the options that you feel will best sell your item, and then contact us to get started. It's easy, it's affordable, and it's a great way to get your items sold.
